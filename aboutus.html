About Us

Welcome to Dope Bricks, the universe where you will find LEGO news, contests and galleries. I am Miruna Manoila and I was corupted by my SO to become addicted to LEGO. He is a massive fan and has a rather huge collection, if you ask him, it is insignificant to its full potential. At first, I helped him with the dirty work, a.k.a. sorting the LEGO, but now, here I am handling the big stuff, MY OWN LEGO FAN SITE. Yes, we are technically grown-ups, but proud to call ourselves AFOL.

The site was born in 2018, due to the undying passion and dire need to keep up with LEGO news and awesome, worldwide LEGO work and now we get to share all this info with you. We try to offer the followers the latest LEGO news available.

More than that, we have contests monthly, where you can win all sorts of cool lego stuff. You can submit it @ Contact section and we promise to review it with the upmost attention.

We hope you enjoy every visit on Dope Bricks and we shall treasure you forever!
